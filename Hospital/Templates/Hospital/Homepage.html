Our Services

Accident detection using CCTV is an intelligent and automated system designed to identify traffic accidents by analyzing video footage captured by surveillance cameras deployed in public and private areas. This cutting-edge system harnesses advanced technologies such as computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning to monitor and analyze real-time video feeds. By detecting unusual events like vehicle collisions, rollovers, sudden halts, or erratic driving patterns, the system aims to improve road safety and reduce the time taken to respond to accidents. The system is trained to recognize specific accident scenarios by processing large datasets of annotated traffic footage, ensuring high accuracy in detection and minimal false alarms. Once an accident is detected, it can instantly alert emergency services, nearby hospitals, and relevant authorities with precise location details and additional contextual information. Additionally, this technology can be integrated with smart city infrastructure and IoT devices, enabling traffic signal control or dynamic rerouting to prevent further congestion around the accident site. With its ability to operate 24/7 and deliver timely responses, this system not only aids in saving lives but also supports post-accident investigations by providing crucial video evidence. Its scalable design makes it suitable for deployment in urban areas, highways, and even remote locations, contributing significantly to safer and smarter transportation systems.
